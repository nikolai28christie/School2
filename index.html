Campus Life

Franklin Academy Sunrise

I had visited in December 2018

Our spacious 10 acre campus is located at 4500 NW 103 Ave., Sunrise, FL 33351 and serves students in kindergarten through eighth grade. Our newly constructed 105,575 square foot state-of-the-art facility includes specialty rooms for music, art, computer labs, large multipurpose room as well as science labs and locker rooms. Franklin students enjoy a beautiful campus with athletic fields and jogging paths with fitness stations located throughout. We provide separate age-appropriate play areas for younger grade levels.
